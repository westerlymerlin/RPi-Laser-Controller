TS Technologies – Privacy Policy (UK GDPR Compliant)

1. Introduction
TS Technologies (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.
We design and manufacture scientific analytic control systems. Our website and products are intended for business‑to‑business use.

2. What Personal Data We Collect
TS Technologies does not actively collect, store, or process personal data through our website or products.
We do not:
- Use cookies
- Use analytics tools
- Use tracking technologies
- Collect personal data through forms
- Conduct marketing profiling

The only personal data we may receive is information you voluntarily provide, such as when you:
- Email us directly
- Contact us by phone
- Request technical support

This typically includes your name, email address, and any information you choose to share.

3. How We Use Personal Data
If you contact us, we use your information solely to:
- Respond to your enquiry
- Provide technical or customer support
- Maintain necessary business correspondence

We do not use your data for marketing or share it with third parties for advertising.

4. Legal Basis for Processing
Where we process personal data you provide, we do so under:
- Legitimate interests – to respond to enquiries and operate our business
- Contract – when communication relates to providing our products or services

5. Sharing Your Personal Data
We do not sell or share personal data.
We may share information only when necessary with:
- Service providers who support our business operations (e.g., email hosting)
- Regulators or authorities if required by law

6. International Transfers
If any service providers operate outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards such as:
- UK adequacy regulations
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)

7. Data Retention
We retain correspondence only as long as necessary to:
- Respond to your enquiry
- Maintain business records
- Meet legal or regulatory obligations

8. Your Rights
Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction or deletion
- Object to processing
- Restrict processing
- Withdraw consent (if ever applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

9. Security
We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect any personal data you provide

10. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle personal data, please contact:
TS Technologies
35 Mill Lane, Broomfield, Essex, UK
Email: info@tstechnologies.co.uk

11. Updates to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
